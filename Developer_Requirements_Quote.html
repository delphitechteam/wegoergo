Project Overview

Business

  • Company: WegoErgo
  • Industry: Commercial ergonomic furniture and ergonomic assessments
  • Location: Livermore Valley, CA (serving Bay Area)
  • Target Audience: B2B (healthcare facilities, corporate offices, education, government, small businesses)

Project Goal

Complete website redesign and build on Squarespace platform. The site will position WegoErgo as an expert ergonomic consultant who represents top furniture manufacturers, NOT an ecommerce store.

Key Business Model

  • Professional ergonomic assessments, program reviews, and training (main services)
  • Representing commercial furniture manufacturers (OM Seating, LoneStar, Workrite, etc.)
  • Education partnership with Worksite International for training programs
  • Curated "Top Dozen" product recommendations (NOT full catalog)
  • Personalized fitting process to avoid costly returns
  • NOT selling products directly online
  • All conversions lead to consultation/quote requests

Platform & Technical Requirements

Platform

  • Squarespace (client preference)
  • Business Plan or higher (for advanced features)
  • Custom domain: www.wegoergo.com

Technical Specifications

  • Responsive design: Mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Browser compatibility: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge (latest versions)
  • Page load speed: Optimized images, under 3 seconds load time
  • SSL certificate: HTTPS required
  • SEO optimized: Meta tags, alt text, proper heading structure
  • Analytics: Google Analytics integration
  • Forms: Contact form with email notifications and auto-reply

Performance Requirements

  • Mobile-responsive on all pages
  • Fast page loading (optimized images)
  • Clean URL structure
  • Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
  • Image lazy loading where appropriate

Design Requirements

Branding & Style

Color Palette:
- Primary Brand Color: #2C5F7C (deep blue) - or client to provide
- CTA/Accent Color: #E67E50 (warm orange) - or client to provide
- Text Dark: #333333
- Text Light/Gray: #666666
- Background Light: #F5F5F5
- White: #FFFFFF

Typography:
- Headers: Bold, modern sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins, or similar)
- H1: 48px desktop / 32px mobile
- H2: 36px desktop / 28px mobile
- H3: 24px desktop / 20px mobile
- Body Text: Clean, readable sans-serif (Inter, Open Sans, or similar)
- 18px desktop / 16px mobile
- Links: Semi-bold, blue (#2C5F7C), underline on hover

Design Style:
- Clean, professional, modern
- Lots of white space
- Clear visual hierarchy
- NOT overly corporate - approachable
- Healthcare/wellness-focused aesthetic

Layout Specifications

  • Max content width: 1200px (centered)
  • Section padding: 80px top/bottom (desktop), 40px (mobile)
  • Grid system: Squarespace's built-in grid
  • Breakpoints: Desktop (1200px+), Tablet (768-1199px), Mobile (below 768px)

Other Forms:

Call-to-Action (CTA) Buttons

Primary CTA:
- Text: "Schedule Free Consultation"

- Links to: Contact page
- Appears on: Every page (hero section, final CTA section)

Secondary CTA:
- Style: Outline (border only), blue text
- Various text depending on context
- Examples: "Explore Our Services", "Learn More", "Request Quote"

Button Specifications:
- Desktop: 14px vertical padding, 28px horizontal padding
- Mobile: Full width, 44px minimum height (thumb-friendly)
- Border radius: 6px
- Hover effect: Darken 10%, lift 2px
- Transition: 0.3s ease
